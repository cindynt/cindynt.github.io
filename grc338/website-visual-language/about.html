At Bottomdrawer, we combine the quality and craftsmanship of our grandparents’ generation with our drive for independence, function, and stylish sustainability. It all results in a collection of tools curated from around the world, that help you do your best work, wherever you are.

Our store offers a carefully selected range of stationery and homegoods that are both beautiful and functional. Whether you're a professional artist, a student, or simply looking for ways to organize your workspace, Bottomdrawer has something to offer. Our collection of notebooks, pens, pencils, and other writing instruments are designed to inspire creativity and productivity, while our storage solutions and desk accessories make it easy to keep everything organized for at home or on the go.

At Bottomdrawer, we understand that our customers have different tastes and needs. That's why we offer a variety of styles and designs to suit different preferences. Whether you prefer classic and timeless designs, bold and colorful patterns, or minimalist and modern aesthetics, you'll find something that speaks to you at our store. Our products are not only functional but also beautiful, making them great gifts for yourself or your loved ones.
